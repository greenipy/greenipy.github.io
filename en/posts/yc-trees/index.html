Carbon uptake of street trees in Yeongcheon City

  • yeongcheon
  • visualisation

The carbon uptake of street trees in Yeongju City is visualised in Carbon uptake of street trees in Yeongcheon City.

The street trees in Yeongcheon City are absorbing about 9,000 tonnes of carbon.

Using the data1), we calculated the carbon uptake of street trees in Yeongcheon City, which accounts for about 1.4% of Gyeongbuk’s street trees2). The carbon uptake of street trees by administrative district in Yeongcheon City is shown on the map.

  1. Open Government Data Portal Gyeongsangbuk-do Yeongcheon-si_Street tree information
  2. Open Government Data Portal Korea Forest Service_Street Tree Establishment Status (as of 2019, by province)
